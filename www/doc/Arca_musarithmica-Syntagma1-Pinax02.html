Arca_musarithmica.Syntagma1.Pinax02

Description

Sub-structures used by Arca_musarithmica to build Kircher's ark. This pinax (s1p2) is for phrases with short penultimate syllables. It contains five columns.

It may be used with any mode except IV and V. II and VI are especially recommended.
