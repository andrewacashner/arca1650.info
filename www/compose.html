Arca musarithmica Athanasii Kircherii MDCL

A digital implementation of Athanasius Kircher's device for automatic music composition from his Musurgia universalis (Rome, 1650), Book VIII.

Implemented in Haskell by Andrew Cashner (Rochester, New York, 2021).

Compose new music using the ark!

In this web app you have two choices:

  • Choose a prepared text

    • Choose from a set of input texts with pre-selected options for mode and meter.
    • You can still choose florid or simple style.
    • Several of the prepared texts include multiple sections with changes of mode and meter.

  • Make the musical choices yourself

    • Choose from prepared input texts and apply your own choices of style, mode, and meter.
